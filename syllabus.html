Curriculum Map

本學程為兩年共四學期。
分為兩個主軸：「專業領域」和「AI素養」。
課程圍繞四個主題式課群，並從核心至進階，再到實作應用。
  • 護理創新專業：聚焦在護理創新專業與 AI 理論發展，建立護理專業與科技連結的理論基礎。
  • AI概念：了解AI概念和VR、AR的基礎，學習python程式語言。
  • AI進階：應用機器學習模型解決實際問題，包含深度學習、影像處理；設計VR模擬訓練與教材，進行對話式AI開發。
  • AI技術實務：與業界及臨床導師合作完成臨床需求導向的原型成果，舉辦成果展。
This program spans two years and four semesters.
It is divided into two main axes: "Professional Field" and "AI Literacy".
Courses revolve around four thematic clusters, progressing from core to advanced and practical application.
  • Nursing Innovation: Focuses on the development of nursing innovation and AI theory, establishing a theoretical foundation linking nursing and technology.
  • AI Concepts: Covers basic AI concepts, VR/AR fundamentals, and Python programming.
  • Advanced AI: Applies machine learning models to real-world problems, including deep learning and image processing; designs VR simulation training and develops conversational AI.
  • AI Practice: Collaborates with industry and clinical mentors to complete prototype projects based on clinical needs and holds achievement exhibitions.

課程特色
Features

1. 聯合師資：
 整合高醫人工智慧生醫研究院、護理學系、醫務管理暨醫療資訊學系、基礎科學教育中心， 同時與校外的陽明交通大學人工智慧技術與應用碩士學位學程、台灣大專院校人工智慧學程聯盟合作。

每學期召開「課群教師協調會議」，一同規劃課程進度、整合跨課程教學活動與學生輔導策略。
設立「跨域共授制度」，如機器學習課程由 AI 教師講授理論，護理教師搭配臨床應用導引。

1. Joint Faculty:
 This program brings together faculty from the Kaohsiung Medical University AI Biomedical Research Center, Department of Nursing, Department of Healthcare Administration and Medical Informatics, and the Center for Basic Science Education. It also collaborates with external partners, including the National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University Master's Program in AI Technology and Applications, as well as the Taiwan University AI Program Alliance.

Each semester, a "Curriculum Coordination Meeting" is held to align course schedules, integrate cross-disciplinary teaching activities, and develop strategies for student guidance.
A "Cross-domain Co-teaching System" is implemented—for example, in a Machine Learning course, AI faculty teach the theoretical concepts while nursing faculty provide guidance on clinical applications.

2. 實作導向課程設計：
  • 產學共創：問題導向專題將會與優必達、宏達國際電子，以及附院合作，利用所學到臨床照護情境中。
  • 跨域合作：根據臨床實際應用情境，與資訊、設計、醫療產業專家合作，分組時採跨學系分組合作、師生共學模式。
  • 成果展演：將有數個成果產出，如：虛擬教案設計、第四學期的開發成果等
2. Practice-oriented Curriculum Design:
  • Industry-Academia Collaboration: Problem-based projects will be carried out in collaboration with companies such as UBTECH Robotics, HTC, and affiliated hospitals, applying learned concepts to real clinical care scenarios.
  • Cross-disciplinary Cooperation: Based on real clinical applications, students will collaborate with experts in IT, design, and the healthcare industry. Group work is organized across departments, fostering a co-learning environment for both faculty and students.
  • Showcasing Outcomes: Multiple outputs will be produced, such as virtual lesson plan designs and development results in the fourth semester.
3. 支援措施：
  • 課程博覽日：每學期初舉辦，包含課程介紹攤位、教師簡介、學生成果展，協助學生規劃修課路徑。
  • 修課支持：課群識別機制、學習歷程地圖表幫助選課，進度追蹤工具、Line官方帳號修課提醒推播。
  • 助教與業師：AI背景的研究型助教；業師定期進班指導或參與成果評審，每學期至少 2 次諮詢或專題指導機會。
    舉辦AI TA訓練營，學習Python輔導技巧、模型教學等。
  • 工作坊與參訪活動：鼓勵教師進行產業參訪與共同產出型教材開發，舉辦AI增能工作坊、設計思考工作坊、教學創新共備工作坊等。
3. Support Services：
  • Course Expo: Held at the beginning of each semester, featuring course introduction booths, faculty presentations, and student project showcases to assist students in planning their course paths.
  • Course Support: Tools such as program identification mechanisms and learning pathway maps help students select courses. Progress tracking tools and official Line account notifications provide course reminders.
  • Teaching Assistants and Industry Mentors: Research-oriented TAs with AI backgrounds; industry mentors regularly join classes or participate in project evaluations, offering at least two consultation or project guidance opportunities per semester.
    AI TA training camps are organized to teach Python tutoring techniques, model instruction, and other skills.
  • Workshops and Field Visits: Faculty are encouraged to conduct industry visits and co-develop instructional materials. AI empowerment workshops, design thinking workshops, and teaching innovation workshops are held regularly.
4. 課程推動：
 (1) 完成課群將發行數位學習歷程證書作為證明。
(2) 完成修課學分後參加成果分享競賽，優勝者可獲得獎金以資鼓勵。
(3) 由產業與臨床導師共同組成跨域輔導小組，支持學生專題。
4. Curriculum Implementation:
 (1) Upon completing the program, students will receive a digital Learning Pathway Certificate as official recognition.
(2) After earning the required credits, students can participate in a project showcase competition, with prizes awarded to outstanding entries.
(3) Cross-disciplinary mentoring teams, composed of industry and clinical instructors, provide guidance and support for student projects.
